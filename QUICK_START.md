## ğŸ” Grammar Issues Explained

### High Severity (Red) - Fix These!
**Subject-Verb Disagreement**
- Wrong: "He are going"
- Right: "He is going"

### Medium Severity (Yellow) - Usually Fix
**Article Mistakes**
- Wrong: "a apple"
- Right: "an apple"

**Mixed Tenses**
- Wrong: "I go and was eating"
- Right: "I go and eat"

### Low Severity (Blue) - Consider Fixing
**Capitalization**
- Wrong: "monday is here"
- Right: "Monday is here"

**Spacing**
- Wrong: "Hello  world"
- Right: "Hello world"
